About Me

Where mathematics meets creative vision

I'm a graduate researcher pursuing my MSc in Computational Mathematics at Kathmandu University, Nepal — supported by a University Grant Commission Scholarship. My research focuses on mathematical modeling of disease dynamics, optimization, and computational methods.

Beyond the equations, I'm a passionate photographer capturing the landscapes, people, and moments that make Nepal extraordinary. I believe the analytical precision of mathematics and the creative instinct of photography complement each other — both seek patterns, structure, and beauty.

Previously, I spent over a year as a Data Analyst at Numeric Mind, working on clinical data pipelines using SAS, R, and Python within CDISC standards for regulatory submissions.
